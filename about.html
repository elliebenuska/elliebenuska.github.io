Ellie Benuska

Work

Ellie Benuska is an illustrator living and working in Los Angeles. After earning a degree in Fine Arts from USC, Ellie moved to New York to pursue a career at Vogue Magazine. With a few years of invaluable education from some of the greatest minds in the industry in her back pocket, Ellie decided to leave the publication and move back to LA to pursue her true passion: illustrating.

Since then Ellie has developed a name for herself in the fashion illustration world, naming Vogue, Who What Wear, and CFDA as a few of her clients. Her style is highly adaptable and ranges in technique. She draws inspiration from fresh faced models, up and coming designers, and backstage beauty at fashion week.
